What is NeuroGen?

NeuroGen is an open‑source research framework that replaces traditional gradient‑based learning with a suite of evolutionary and local‑learning mechanisms. It supports:

  • Evolutionary search over neural architectures
  • Genome‑driven network construction
  • Hebbian, Oja, BCM, STDP learning rules
  • Dynamic topology mutations (add/remove nodes & edges)

How It Works

Evolutionary Search

Populations of genomes are evaluated on a fitness function and evolve via selection, crossover, and mutation.

Genome‑Driven Construction

Each genome encodes a neural topology; decoding produces a functional network ready for learning.

Local Learning Rules

Synapses adapt with biologically‑inspired rules (Hebbian, Oja, BCM, STDP) without gradients.
